Radon Testing

Test for radioactive gas believed to cause lung cancer

  • Radon gas travels up through soil and can become trapped in the home
  • Confirmed high radon is fixable for nearly every home
  • Portland, NW Oregon, the Columbia Gorge, Willamette River Valley tend to be areas of concern for radon exposure
  • World Health Organization recommends mitigation for radon level 2.7 pCi/L or above
